# RESTORED OS: ABOUT THE PROJECT

## THE DISCOVERY

In early 2024, a collection of unmarked CD-Rs and MiniDisc recordings surfaced at a Brooklyn estate sale. The metadata led to this: **Whip Montez** - a name that appears in exactly zero search results, no Wikipedia entries, no streaming catalogs. Just raw recordings, handwritten tracklists, and fragments of a career that seemingly never existed outside these files.

*Or did it?*

## WHAT IS RESTORED OS?

**Restored OS** is an Alternative Reality Experience (ARE) - a digital preservation project that exists in the gray space between documentary and fiction, memory and myth. We've reconstructed what a 2004 artist's website might have looked like if the technology had caught up to her vision.

**Our mission:** Preserve cultural artifacts that fell through the cracks of the early internet. Whether Whip Montez was a underground legend or an elaborate artistic persona, the music exists. The recordings are real. *You* decide what's true.

## THE ALTERNATIVE REALITY EXPERIENCE

**What makes this an ARE?**

1. **Deliberate Ambiguity**: We never confirm or deny authenticity
2. **Real Technology**: Functional AI tools, actual audio engineering
3. **Historical Plausibility**: Every detail researched for 2001-2004 accuracy
4. **Interactive Mystery**: *You* investigate, *you* conclude

**Did Whip Montez exist?**
- The music is real (you're hearing it)
- The era is accurate (verified slang, beats, references)
- The story is coherent (Jari's role, Brooklyn scene, timeline)
- But search engines? Silent.

This is digital archaeology. Restored OS doesn't just tell a story - it *is* the artifact.

## FOR RESEARCHERS & HISTORIANS

**Why preserve "lost" artists?**

The early 2000s were a cultural inflection point:
- Female MCs breaking barriers
- Analog-to-digital transition
- MySpace era fragmentation
- Countless careers that never made it to streaming

Whether Whip Montez was real or represents *hundreds* of real artists lost to time, this project honors that moment. The skills were real. The hustle was real. The music deserved to be heard.

**Historical Preservation Goals:**
- Archive Y2K hip-hop aesthetics before they're forgotten
- Document female MC contributions to boom-bap era
- Preserve early-internet creative culture
- Make "lost media" accessible through modern tech

## TECHNICAL DETAILS (For The Curious)

**Built With:**
- React + Vite for the OS interface
- Firebase for authentication, storage, database
- Google Gemini API for AI text generation
- Audio: HTML5 with Firebase Storage streaming
- Future: Imagen 3 (images), Veo 3 (video)

**Design Philosophy:**
- Windows 98 meets Terminal UI
- Emerald green on black (night-vision aesthetic)
- Glitch effects, scan lines, CRT simulation
- Mobile-responsive retro (it shouldn't work, but it does)

**Privacy & Data:**
- Optional guest mode (no sign-up required)
- Saved lyrics stored locally or in your Firebase account
- No tracking pixels, no ads, no surveillance capitalism
- This isn't a product. It's a preservation project.
